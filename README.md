# AI Interview Assistant 🎙️🤖

An intelligent chat assistant designed to help you during online interviews. It listens to your Google Meet or Zoom calls in real-time, processes the conversation, and provides contextual assistance, hints, and feedback on the fly.

## Features
* **Real-Time Audio Capture:** Seamlessly captures system audio from ongoing meeting platforms (Zoom, Meet, Teams).
* **AI-Powered Analysis:** Transcribes and analyzes the conversation to generate smart, contextual responses.
* **Concurrent Processing:** Built for high performance and low latency to ensure the AI's assistance keeps up with the live conversation.
